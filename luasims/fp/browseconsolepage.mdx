<Info>
  Search Console: **This allows staff/administrators instant access to every student in the server**. To view the student overview page (which also includes individual behaviour management), you must use selective ID and hover over a user and left-click on their avatar or click on the username when hovering over a student
</Info>

Every student has an **individual tab** displayed on a list featuring their username, display name, year group & user/luasims ID
