---
title: "Create Profile"
description: "This is the first page all LuaSIMS users will see if they have not registered with the organisation that the system is within"
---

- Users must enter a valid **<u>Roleplay Name</u>**
- Users must select a **<u>Title</u>** based on the selections available if they are a staff member or administrator
- Users must **create and submit** the profile they are creating

<Info>
  This information **can be changed** via the roleplay name button located at the bottom right of the screen from the staff and student's perspective, or if reset via the **Student Profile** panel, available to staff & administrators.
</Info>
