## Getting started

<Note>

</Note>

## Voice Chat Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Show Voice Chat Service">
    If you cannot see **VoiceChatService** in the explorer window right click the workspace and click **show services**. Select **VoiceChatService** in the menu and at the bottom press **Done**, it will now be visible in the explorer window.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable Audio Api">
    Set the UseAudioApi property to enabled, to ensure voice can be broadcasted correctly through it.

    ![image.png](/Screenshot2025-11-16211241.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Requirements">
    Go to your **Game Settings** and on the **Communication** section ensure Microphone usage is enabled. Within **Game Settings** visit the **Places** tab and adjust the server limit, at maximum it can be **50 players**.
  </Step>
</Steps>
