### Lesson Register Page

Allows staff & teachers to **log an entire class of students** based on three available options (e.g **<u>Absent, Late & Present</u>**). Previous data from other lessons is also displayed

<Info>
  This lesson attendance data is **sent to your discord server** if configured
</Info>
