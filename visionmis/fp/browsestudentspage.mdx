<Info>
  Browse Students: **This allows staff/administrators instant access to every student in the server**. To view a [**student profile**](/visionmis/fp/studentprofile), you must click the <u>View</u> button when hovering over a student
</Info>

**Each student has an individual tab featuring their Username**, Year Group & User/Vision ID
