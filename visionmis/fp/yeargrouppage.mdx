---
title: "Year Group Page"
description: "The Year Group Page is the central hub for viewing all vital data collected for an individual year group. Take a look at our excellent data insight & collection features below:"
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Overview" defaultOpen icon="list">
    This is the primary location for viewing every student in a year group.

    ![Image(33) Pn](/images/image\(33\).png)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Attendance" defaultOpen icon="badge-check">
    This displays the amount of students in the year group (**<u>per session</u>**), the percentage of students in the year group in comparison to the entire school, and an additional list of students with their active attendance status.

    ![Image(34) Pn](/images/image\(34\).png)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Timetable" defaultOpen icon="calendars">
    Displays the current lesson for the year group, the **next lesson**, along with the **current lesson** and its details. The lesson details include: scheduled time, subject, and location. The timetable page also features an **interactive visual timetable** which can be highlighted for the specific year group.

    ![Image(35) Pn](/images/image\(35\).png)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Behaviour" defaultOpen icon="thumbs-up">
    Displays the amount of behaviour points, isolations & merits for all of the students in the year group combined. This page also directs users to the [**Behaviour Dashboard**](/fp/behaviourdashboard) page.

    ![Image(36) Pn](/images/image\(36\).png)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
