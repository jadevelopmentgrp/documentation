title: "Create Profile"
description: "This is the first page all Vision users will see if they have not registered with the organisation that the system is within"
---

- Users must enter a valid **<u>Roleplay Name</u>**
- Users must enter a **<u><Tooltip tip="">Gender</Tooltip></u>** based on the selections available, or their own
- Users must **confirm** the profile they are creating

<Info>
  This information **can **be changed from the student's perspective, or if modified via the **Users & Security** panel, available to staff & administrators.

  \
  The Gender **cannot** be modified after being submitted
</Info>
