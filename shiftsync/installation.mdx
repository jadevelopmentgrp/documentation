## Getting started

<Note>
  You must enable **HTTP Requests** and **API Services** within the Game Settings tab in Roblox studio. This is located under the Security section. Please ensure this is enabled before opening a ticket with our support team
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Drag and drop the .RBXM ShiftSync file into workspace">
    After downloading the product, you will be given a RBXM file. Drag this into Workspace of your gam
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get the sound effects">
    Head to the links below, and click the **Get Audio** button and add them to your inventory. This will allow you to hear them in-game
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your group and role settings">
    Move onto the [group settings](https://docs.jadevelopment.co.uk/shiftsync/configuration#group-settings) to continue
  </Step>
  <Step title="All done!">
    Once you have updated your group settings, you will be able to use the system as it is in its current state
  </Step>
</Steps>

Now, continue onto the rest of the [configuration](https://docs.jadevelopment.co.uk/shiftsync/configuration) process.
